CollabMates


It all started with a call from Nipun Goyal (Co-founder Curofy) who was working on a new idea. He wanted me to build the pilot run app. I found the idea intersting and the people working on it Brilliant and experienced. This made me say yes.

Few months after He called me and asked to work as an Full-time member of the start up. I didn't knew if I was ready for it and how I would manage this along side my college, but there was no way I could have said NO to it.

The Idea

The idea was to build a platform for communities to network more efficiently and allow users to find Relevant communities with ease.

Along with this, the three main objectives were :

  • Making niche communities discoverable and only allowing relevant authenticated members
  • Reduce spam by allowing only useful discussions in your growing community
  • Drive meaningful relationships between members and develop belongingness

My Role

I was assigned the responsibility to desgin the complete flow along with all screens for both Android application and Web. I was required to work from scratch starting from research to making mockups.

Tools Used

We decided to work on Zeplin, It provides all essentials features required for designing mockups and also is a great application for collaborative working. It took me just a day to get familiar with the whole interface it is quite similar to that of Figma and sketch.

Design Approach



I used the double diamond approach for the project. The double diamond approach starts with the exploratory research around the project, tries to gather as much as data possible with the use of a user research method (In our case, user interviews) to define the scope of the project. Once the scope is defined, multiple solutions are evaluated and tested against the user to arrive on the final solution.

Competitor Analysis

There are many competitors in the market but there is none providing much similar features.
Some companies targeting same users are:

Facebook

Facebook does have a lot of group centric features, but they are more focused on individual user.

Meetup

Meetup has quite the same moto, as of ours. But they only have location based grouping and have a completely different methodology than that of ours.

WhatsApp

WhatsApp is only focused on chat groups and doesn't provide many special privileges to group Admin.

Designing initial wireframes

Making a user signup to your platform is the most important and difficult task to do and for this to happen an appropriate landing page is very essential. The design should guide the user to the and also provide all the required information to make a decision. To increase conversions stats (like companies associated, testimonial etc.) to win a users trust is very much important. Making the landing page visually appealing is also very essential. Knowing the fact that a good UI helps in increasing conversions I tried to design it with the latest UI trends.
